Hello, I am Hery Harinony. I am a Malagasy and I am know a Graphic Design Student at Mauritius. Before Graphic Design, I was studying Architecture et Madagascar and have got the 2nd years DTS Certificate. I like every kind of Design. I’m quite fond of strong colors and textures, but I try to use a wide range of visual styles to always match the tone of the
project I’m working on. In my time off I draw and I paint. I am interested in all sort of art and technologie. One of my ongoing project is designing a small experimental game with some Malagasy friends. I hope you will enjoy your visite!
